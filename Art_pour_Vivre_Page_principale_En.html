About Us

At Art pour Vivre, we specialize in creating and organizing unique and innovative temporary exhibitions across France. From the street to the museum, our exhibitions adapt to various spaces and attract a wide audience, from enthusiasts to passionate art lovers.

Collaborations and partnerships

We collaborate with emerging and renowned artists as well as brands looking to showcase their collections. Through strategic partnerships with scientific and technological institutions, we offer immersive and interactive exhibitions..

Learn more→

Our services

We offer various services, including guided tours, art classes, the simplification of artistic concepts, as well as workshops and educational projects. Our offerings are designed to be interactive and immersive, providing a unique experience for every visitor. We offer discounted rates for students as well as special offers for school groups. Our educational programs allow students to discover art interactively and even create and exhibit their own works.
